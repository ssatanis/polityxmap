Rural Telehealth Expansion

Ithaca, NY, USA

Expanding high‑bandwidth telehealth services to six Cayuga County towns.

Telehealth Rural Health Access
Author Sahaj Satani
Institution Cornell University

Background

Despite high health literacy, rural residents in Tompkins County have limited access to specialists.

Policy Overview

Deploying mobile telehealth vans equipped with satellite uplink for instant access to remote providers.

Key Stakeholders

Cayuga Health System, local clinics, internet providers, state government.

Cost Analysis

$500,000 infrastructure; $250,000 annual staffing

Success Metrics

40% increase in telehealth usage within 1 year

Timeline

Rollout begins Q1 2026 with completion by Q4 2026
